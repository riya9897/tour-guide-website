Chennakeshava Temple, India

About Chennakeshava Temple

Chennakeshava Temple is a magnificent example of Hoysala architecture, located in Halebidu, Karnataka. It is known for its intricate carvings and historical significance, showcasing the artistic and architectural brilliance of the Hoysala dynasty.

Top Attractions

  • Chennakeshava Temple
  • Halebidu Archaeological Site
  • Hoysaleshwara Temple
  • Kalleshwara Temple
  • Shivaganga Temple

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Chennakeshava Temple is from October to March when the weather is pleasant, allowing visitors to explore the temple and its surroundings comfortably.
