Jatayu Earth's center, India

About Jatayu Rock

Jatayu Earth's Center is a unique rock formation located in the Western Ghats of Kerala, India. It is renowned for its mythological significance and is home to the world's largest bird sculpture, representing Jatayu from the Indian epic Ramayana.

Top Attractions

  • Jatayu Sculpture
  • Adventure Activities
  • Nature Trails
  • Panoramic Views
  • Mythological Significance

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Jatayu Rock is from October to March when the weather is cool and pleasant, making it ideal for outdoor activities and sightseeing.
