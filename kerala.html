Kerala Backwaters

About Kerala Backwaters

Kerala Backwaters are a network of lagoons, lakes, and canals that stretch along the Arabian Sea coast of Kerala. Known for their serene beauty and traditional houseboat experiences, the backwaters offer a unique perspective on Kerala's natural and cultural heritage.

Top Attractions

  • Alleppey Houseboats
  • Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary
  • Vembanad Lake
  • Pathiramanal Island
  • Marari Beach

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit the Kerala Backwaters is from November to February when the weather is cool and pleasant, making it ideal for houseboat cruises and exploring the scenic beauty.
