Hampi, India

About Hampi

Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage site located in the southern Indian state of Karnataka. It was once the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire and is renowned for its ancient temples, palaces, and stunning landscapes. The ruins of Hampi are spread over a vast area and showcase the architectural brilliance of the Vijayanagara period.

Top Attractions

  • Virupaksha Temple
  • Vittala Temple and Stone Chariot
  • Hampi Bazaar
  • Lotus Mahal
  • Elephant Stables

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Hampi is during the cooler months from October to March when the weather is pleasant for exploring the ruins and outdoor activities.
